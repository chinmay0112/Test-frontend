1. Introduction

Welcome to PrepMaster ("we," "our," or "us"). We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website.

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our services. By accessing or using PrepMaster, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us.

Personal Info

Name, email address, phone number, and password credential.

Payment Data

Transaction details for purchases (processed securely via Razorpay).

Usage Data

Test scores, time taken, questions attempted, and performance analytics.

Social Login

Basic profile info from Google/Facebook if you use social sign-in.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Service Delivery: To generate test reports, rank analysis, and provide personalized study recommendations.
  • Account Management: To facilitate account creation, login, and profile management.
  • Communication: To send you important updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. This includes encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and secure hashing for passwords (PBKDF2/Argon2). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers; payments are processed by PCI-DSS compliant providers.
